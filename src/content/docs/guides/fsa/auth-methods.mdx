Integrate various authentication methods to allow users to sign into your application. Choose from the following options:

<CardGrid>
  <LinkCard
    title="Passwordless"
    href="/guides/fsa/passwordless/"
    description="Users sign in by verifying a one-time password (OTP) or a secure magic link sent to their email."
  />
  <LinkCard
    title="Social logins"
    href="/guides/fsa/social-logins/"
    description="Users sign in using their existing accounts from popular social media platforms like Google or GitHub."
  />
  <LinkCard
    title="Enterprise SSO"
    href="/guides/fsa/enterprise-sso/"
    description="Users sign in with their organization's Single Sign-On (SSO) credentials."
  />
</CardGrid>



Once you've chosen your preferred authentication method, Scalekit provides a seamless sign-in experience through our managed authentication pages. We handle all the complex identity verification processes and securely return the authenticated user's profile to your application.
