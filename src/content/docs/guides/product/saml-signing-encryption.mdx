Enterprise customers often require SAML-based authentication to let their employees sign in to your application using their company's identity provider (IdP).

Your application already supports SAML authentication if you've set up an SSO connection during your [Scalekit authentication setup](/manual/sso-quickstart/).

In this guide you will learn about few more advanced features that you can use to strengthen your SAML-based Single Sign-On (SSO) connections.
