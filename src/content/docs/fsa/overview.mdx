Developers use Scalekit's Full Stack authentication to rapidly add enterprise-ready authentication, user management, user roles, enterprise SSO, and SCIM provisioning to their B2B SaaS apps.

Here's a quick overview of what you can do with Scalekit:

- Offer passwordless, social, and enterprise-SSO sign-inâ€”and let each organization choose which methods its members may use.
- Provision accounts automatically through sign up, Just-In-Time (JIT) or SCIM directory sync.
- Enforce per-organization session policies such as maximum duration and idle-timeout. Invite, remove, and assign roles to users with a ready-made admin UI or API.
- Trigger secure, global logout for individual users or as per organization administrators via their identity provider dashboard.
